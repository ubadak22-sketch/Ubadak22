iPhone 16 & 17 Series Repair Guide

iPhone 16 Mini Repair Guide (Screen & Battery)

Screen Repair

Tools Needed: Pentalobe screwdriver, Y000 screwdriver, suction cup, spudger, tweezers, new screen.

  1. Power off the phone.
  2. Remove the 2 bottom screws near the Lightning port.
  3. Place suction cup near the bottom, gently lift screen while using spudger to release clips.
  4. Open phone like a book (hinge on the side).
  5. Disconnect battery connector first.
  6. Disconnect screen connectors (usually 2–3 flat cables).
  7. Transfer front camera, Face ID, earpiece from old screen if needed.
  8. Connect new screen carefully.
  9. Reconnect battery.
  10. Test the screen before sealing.
  11. Close the phone, secure screws.

Battery Replacement

Tools Needed: Same + adhesive strips.

  1. Power off.
  2. Open screen (same steps as above).
  3. Disconnect battery connector first.
  4. Remove screws and bracket holding battery.
  5. Gently lift battery using spudger or adhesive pull tabs.
  6. Place new battery with adhesive strips.
  7. Reconnect battery connector.
  8. Test phone.
  9. Close screen and secure screws.

Mini has tiny space, so cables are fragile. Don’t force anything.

iPhone 16 & 16 Pro Repair Guide (Screen & Battery)

Screen Repair

Tools and steps are same as Mini, but screen is larger. Pro models have extra LiDAR scanner cable—disconnect carefully. Front camera and Face ID sensors must be transferred to new screen.

Battery Replacement

Larger battery than Mini, same procedure. Battery may be slightly curved; remove slowly. Pro battery connector may have extra shielding screws, keep track.

iPhone 16 Pro Max Repair Guide (Screen & Battery)

Screen Repair

Same general steps. Largest screen, so extra care for flex cables. LiDAR sensor and Pro cameras are delicate; do not bend connectors. Front assembly transfer same as Pro.

Battery Replacement

Biggest battery. Same adhesive method. Extra caution with internal screws, there are more than smaller models.

Tips for All iPhone 16 Models

  • Always disconnect battery first to avoid shorting.
  • Keep screws organized; different screws have different lengths.
  • Test everything before sealing.
  • Use plastic tools to avoid scratching internals.
  • Genuine parts = safer; aftermarket parts = cheaper but may cause errors.
  • Take pictures while opening if you forget placement.

iPhone 17 Series Repair Guide (Screen & Battery)

Screen Replacement

Tools Needed: Pentalobe (P2) screwdriver, Y000 screwdriver, suction cup or iSlack tool, plastic spudger, tweezers, new screen assembly.

  1. Power off the device to prevent any electrical issues.
  2. Remove the two Pentalobe screws near the charging port.
  3. Apply heat to the bottom edge of the device to soften the adhesive.
  4. Use a suction cup to gently lift the screen, creating a gap.
  5. Insert a plastic spudger into the gap to separate the screen from the body.
  6. Disconnect the battery to ensure safety during the repair.
  7. Remove any screws securing the display connectors and detach them.
  8. Transfer necessary components (e.g., front camera, Face ID sensor) to the new screen.
  9. Connect the new screen and reassemble the device.

Note: For models with Face ID, ensure that the new screen is properly paired with the device to maintain functionality.

Battery Replacement

Tools Needed: Pentalobe (P2) screwdriver, Y000 screwdriver, plastic spudger, tweezers, adhesive remover (if necessary), new battery.

  1. Power off the device to prevent any electrical issues.
  2. Open the device following the screen replacement steps.
  3. Disconnect the battery to ensure safety during the repair.
  4. Remove screws securing the battery bracket and detach it.
  5. Use a plastic spudger to gently lift the battery from its compartment.
  6. Clean the battery compartment to remove any residual adhesive.
  7. Place the new battery into the compartment and secure it.
  8. Reconnect the battery and reassemble the device.

Note: Apple has introduced a new battery adhesive in recent models. If the adhesive is difficult to remove, consider using a USB-powered tool designed for this purpose.

Model-Specific Considerations

  • iPhone 17 & iPhone 17 Mini: Screen Size: 6.1" and 5.4" respectively. Design: Similar to previous models, but with slight internal layout changes. Battery Capacity: Approximately 3,200mAh for the iPhone 17 and 2,900mAh for the Mini.
  • iPhone 17 Pro: Screen Size: 6.3". Design: Features additional components like the LiDAR scanner. Battery Capacity: Approximately 3,800mAh.
  • iPhone 17 Pro Max: Screen Size: 6.7". Design: Largest model with more internal components. Battery Capacity: Approximately 4,800mAh.

Note: The Pro and Pro Max models have more complex internal layouts. Take extra care when handling internal components to avoid damage.

Repair Tips

  • Use Genuine Parts: Always opt for genuine Apple parts to ensure compatibility and maintain device integrity.
  • Handle with Care: Be cautious when dealing with internal components, especially the Face ID sensor and battery connectors.
  • Test Before Sealing: After replacing the screen or battery, power on the device to test functionality before fully reassembling.
  • Dispose of Old Parts Properly: Recycle old batteries and screens at certified e-waste disposal centers.
